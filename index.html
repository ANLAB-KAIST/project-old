Welcome to the list of open-source projects from ANLAB, KAIST!

Packet Processing Frameworks

NBA (Network Balancing Act)

  • Repository: (under preparation) https://github.com/ANLAB-KAIST/NBA
  • Paper: (to appear in ACM EuroSys 2015)
  • License: MIT License
A high-performance packet processing framework for heterogeneous processors.

DoubleClick

  • Repository: (under preparation)
  • Paper: ACM APSys Workshop 2012 [pdf]
  • License: Click License (combination of GPL for codes dervied from Linux and MIT-like license for the rest)
A batch-oriented derivation of the Click modular rotuer on top of PSIO.

PacketShader IO Engine

  • Repository: https://github.com/ANLAB-KAIST/Packet-IO-Engine
  • Paper: ACM SIGCOMM 2010 [pdf]
  • Used by: XIA
  • License: GPLv2
A batch-oriented packet I/O driver derived from the Intel ixgbe Linux driver.

PacketShader

  • Repository: (not public yet)
  • Paper: ACM SIGCOMM 2010 [pdf]
  • License: (tentative)
A simple GPU-accelerated packet processing application framework.

Educational Networking Frameworks

KENSv3

  • Repository: (under preparation)
  • License: (tentative)
The next-generation event-based simulator and TCP/IP hands-on assignments on top of it.

KENSv2

  • Repository: https://github.com/ANLAB-KAIST/KENSv2
  • Paper: ACM SIGCSE 2015 [ACM Digital Library]
  • License: (tentative)
A polished TCP programming assignments based on KENSv1

KENSv1

  • Repository: https://github.com/ANLAB-KAIST/KENSv1
  • License: (tentative)
A bug-fixed version of the original KENS (KAIST educational networking framework) developed by NC Lab.
